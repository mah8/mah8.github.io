Eight-Ba International Business Development

Based in Singapore, EIGHT-BA is an Asia-centric Business Development Consultancy focusing on connecting business to emerging markets world wide. We have decades of experience in helping businesses navigate the complex and ever-changing global market

Number eight holds a significant symbolic meaning in both Eastern and Western cultures. In Western culture, the number eight is associated with infinity, as it resembles the infinity symbol when turned on its side

In Chinese calligraphy, the character for Ba (number eight) is formed by two gracefully ascending strokes that seem to flow into one another, embodying balance and harmony

Together in our name and logo, they serve as an enduring reminder to find balance and harmony in the infinite journey of growth and success

Whether you're a small startup or a large corporation, we can provide the guidance and support you need to achieve your goals. With EIGHT-BA, you are assured of pragmatic bespoke advice, based on real relationships and addressing your specific needs

How can we help?

Business development: We'll help you establish new partnerships and expand into new markets through direct sales, distribution, licensing, and joint ventures. Our extensive network and experience in international markets will open doors for you that would otherwise be difficult to access.

International expansion: We'll guide you through the process of setting up operations in foreign countries and help you navigate cultural and regulatory differences. We'll provide you with the support you need to ensure a smooth transition and minimize the risk of failure.

Sales and marketing: We'll help you develop a strong sales and marketing strategy to promote your products and services in international markets. We'll work with you to create effective marketing materials and campaigns that will help you stand out in a crowded global market.

Strategic planning: We'll work closely with you to develop a comprehensive plan for growth and success in international markets. We'll help you identify and prioritize the most promising markets, and map out a clear path to success.

Market research and analysis: We'll provide you with in-depth insights on your target markets, identifying new opportunities, understanding the competitive landscape and assessing the potential of your products and services. This will allow you to make informed decisions and avoid costly mistakes.
