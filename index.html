EIGHTBA

Football Consulting

Based in Singapore, EIGHTBA is an Asia-centric Football Consultancy firm. We aim to transform football in Asia through our thought leadership, in-depth and hands-on experience, as well as exclusive access to a global network of reputable football partners.

We work closely with key stakeholders to develop best-in-class solutions throughout the entire football business value chain. Our areas of expertise include, but are not limited to, investments in clubs in major leagues, talent development and management, media engagement and marketing, as well as sports technology.

With EIGHTBA, you are assured of pragmatic bespoke advice, tailored according to your specific needs.
