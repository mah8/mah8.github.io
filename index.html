Eight-Ba International Business Development

Number eight holds a significant symbolic meaning in both Eastern and Western cultures. In Western culture, the number eight is associated with infinity, as it resembles the infinity symbol when turned on its side. In Chinese culture, the character for the number eight (Ba) is made up of two horizontal lines, which symbolize balance and harmony

The infinity symbol of the Western Eight represents the endless possibilities and potential for growth and change, while the balance and harmony of the Chinese Ba remind us to stay grounded and in alignment with our values as we navigate these possibilities

Together in our name and logo, they serve as a reminder to find balance in the infinite journey of growth and success

Based in Singapore, EIGHT-BA is an Asia-centric Business Development Consultancy focusing on connecting Asian business to emerging markets world wide. We have decades of experience in helping businesses navigate the complex and ever-changing global market

Whether you're a small startup or a large corporation, we can provide the guidance and support you need to achieve your goals. With EIGHT-BA, you are assured of pragmatic bespoke advice, tailored according to your specific needs
