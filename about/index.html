About us



Our Philosophy

Our story begins with a passion for international business development. We believe that with the right guidance, businesses can achieve incredible growth and tap into previously untapped markets. With this vision in mind, we set out to establish ourselves as leading consultants in international business development.

Our success is rooted in our deep understanding of global markets and our commitment to providing well-thought tailored solutions, that meet the unique needs of each client. We take the time to listen to our clients challenges and goals, and work closely with them to develop a comprehensive plan for success. And we don't stop there.

We are always monitoring market trends and adapting our strategies to ensure our clients stay ahead of the competition. We know that success in today's global economy requires a keen understanding of cultural differences, local regulations and practices, localized marketing, operational efficiency, and market dynamics.

We offer the extra power to give you an advantage through every step of your international expansion success!


Eight Ba

Eight is a number of great luck and abundance. Different cultures have celebrated it as signifying victory, success, or achieving goals.

In Western culture, the number eight is associated with infinity, as it resembles the infinity symbol when turned on its side.

In Chinese calligraphy, the character for Ba (number eight) is formed by two gracefully ascending strokes that seem to flow into one another, embodying balance and harmony.

Together in our name, they serve as an enduring reminder to finding balance and harmony in our infinite journey of growth and success.
