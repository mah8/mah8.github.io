Hugo Martins is the founder of EIGHTBA. An experienced Sports Business professional with a track record including landmark corporate, talent transfer and commercial deals across Asia, and involving some of the most famous personalities of World Sports, such as Cristiano Ronaldo, James Rodriguez, or Jose Mourinho.A PhD in Management from Nanyang Technological University in Singapore, and a Sloan Fellow in Leadership and Strategy from the London Business School, his professional and research interests lie in the intersection of competitive success and business viability in the realm of professional sports teams.

What set us apart from the rest? First, EIGHTBA has an in-depth understanding of the football ecosystem, such as the importance of football in the national agenda, the role of football as an enabler of sustainable development goals, the symbiotic relationship between football and media, the economics of football, and the life cycle of a professional footballer. Second, EIGHTBA knows the key success factors of European football, and having been in Asia for close to a decade, EIGHTBA is well-poised to adapt them to the Asian context. Third, EIGHTBA is forward-thinking. Although football is a sport with long tradition, EIGHTBA constantly thinks of incorporating the latest trends and technologies in the projects we embark on. The current pandemic has also prompted us to ponder over the changing dynamics of football given an increasingly socially distanced world with evolving audience profiles.
